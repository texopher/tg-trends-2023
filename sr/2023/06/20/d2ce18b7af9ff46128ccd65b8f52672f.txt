iAlejandro Garnacho Ferreyra (born 1 July 2004), is an Argentine professional
ifootballer who plays as a winger for Premier League club Manchester United
iand
ithe Argentina national team.
i
iGarnacho joined Manchester United's youth system from Atlético Madrid in
iOctober 2020. He won the FA Youth Cup and the Jimmy Murphy Young Player of
ithe
iYear award in May 2022. The month prior, he had made his first-team debut at
ithe age of 17.
i
iGarnacho initially played youth international football for Spain, the country
iof his birth, before making his debut for Argentina at under-20 level in
i2022.
iHe made his debut for the senior Argentina team in June 2023, against
iAustralia.
i
i===== Club career =====
i
iBorn in Madrid, Garnacho joined Atlético Madrid's youth system in 2015 from
iGetafe.[2]
i
iIn October 2020, he joined the Manchester United Academy.[3] United paid
iAtlético a £420,000 fee.[4] He signed his first professional contract with
ithe
iclub in July 2021.[4]
i
iHe came under the spotlight for his solo goal in the FA Youth Cup win over
iEverton, which was nominated for United's Goal of the Month award for
iFebruary
i2022.[5] After being named as an unused substitute for several Premier League
imatches, Garnacho made his first-team debut for United on 28 April, replacing
iAnthony Elanga in the 91st minute of a 1–1 draw against Chelsea.[6] Garnacho
iwon the Jimmy Murphy Young Player of the Year award in May.[7] He scored
itwice
iin the final of the FA Youth Cup against Nottingham Forest on 11 May, helping
iUnited win the competition for the first time since 2011.[8]
i
iFor the start of the 2022–23 Premier League season, he changed his shirt
inumber from 75 to 49.[9] On 4 October, he scored a late winner for United's
iU21 against Barrow in the 2022–23 EFL Trophy.[10] Garnacho made his first
isenior start for United on 27 October, in a 3–0 win over Moldovan team
iSheriff
iTiraspol in the UEFA Europa League, whereafter he was praised by manager Erik
iten Hag for his improvement over the past few weeks, saying that though
ipreviously dissatisfied, he was happy with Garnacho's improved attitude and
iresilience.[11] On 3 November, he scored his first senior goal in a Europa
iLeague match against Real Sociedad.[12] Garnacho scored his first goal in the
iPremier League on 13 November, an injury-time winner against Fulham.[13] On
i14
iJanuary 2023 in the Manchester derby, Garnacho set up Marcus Rashford's 82nd
iminute winner in a 2–1 win against Manchester City.[14] On 14 March 2023,
iGarnacho announced he would be sidelined for several weeks after suffering an
iankle ligament injury during a game against Southampton.[15] On 28 April
i2023,
iGarnacho signed a new contract extension that will last until 30 June
i2028.[16]
i
iOn 13 May 2023, Garnacho returned from two months out with injury, scoring
ithe second goal in a 2–0 victory against Wolves.[17]
i
i===== International career =====
i
iGarnacho is eligible to play for his country of birth, Spain, and Argentina,
ias his mother is Argentine.[18] He made three appearances for the Spain
iunder-18 team in 2021.[19]
i
iOn 7 March 2022, Garnacho was called up to the senior Argentina team as part
iof an initial 44-man squad for their two World Cup qualifiers that month.[20]
iHe made it to the final 33-man squad for the fixtures,[21][22] but did not
imake an appearance in either game.[23]
i
iGarnacho made his debut for the Argentina under-20 side on 26 March 2022,
iwhen he started in a friendly match against the United States.[24] He scored
ifour goals in four appearances[19] for the under-20 team at the 2022 Maurice
iRevello Tournament winning the Revelation Player and Goal of the Tournament
iawards.[25][26]
i
iIn March 2023, he was called up once again to the senior Argentina team for
itwo friendly matches against Panama and Curaçao,[27][28] but had to withdraw
ifrom the squad after suffering an ankle injury.[15] He made his debut for the
isenior team on 15 June 2023, during a friendly against Australia at Workers'
iStadium, coming on as a substitute for Nicolás González during the second
ihalf
iof the game.[29]
