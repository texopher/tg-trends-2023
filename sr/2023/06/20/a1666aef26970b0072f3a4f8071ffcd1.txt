iGedson Carvalho Fernandes (born 9 January 1999) is a Portuguese professional
ifootballer who plays as a midfielder for Turkish Süper Lig club Beşiktaş.
iBorn
iin São Tomé and Príncipe, he represents the Portugal national football team.
i
i===== Club career =====
i
iBorn in São Tomé, Fernandes started his youth career at SC Frielas in
iPortugal in 2008. A year later, he joined S.L. Benfica's youth system, where
ihe progressed to the club's reserve team in 2017. On 11 February that year,
ihe
imade his professional debut with Benfica B in a LigaPro 4–2 win over
iDesportivo das Aves.[4]
i
iFollowing his promotion to Benfica's first team in the 2018–19 season,
iFernandes debuted as a starter in a 1–0 home win over Fenerbahçe in the first
ileg of the UEFA Champions League third qualifying round on 7 August 2018. A
iweek later, in the second leg away, he scored a 26th-minute opener in a 1–1
idraw, which qualified Benfica to the competition's play-off round.[5][6]
i
iFernandes signed on an 18-month loan for Premier League club Tottenham
iHotspur on 15 January 2020.[7] He made his Premier League debut coming on in
ithe 80th minute away against Watford on 18 January, which ended 0–0.[8]
iFernandes was recalled by Benfica on 1 February 2021.[9]
i
iIn January 2021, Fernandes moved to Turkish club Galatasaray, on a loan deal
iuntil the end of the season.[10]
i
iOn 3 February 2022, Turkish club Beşiktaş announced a deal to purchase
iFernandes. Since the club already had met their maximum quota of foreign
iplayers, his contract was made to start on 1 July 2022. Until then, Benfica
iloaned him to fellow Süper Lig club Çaykur Rizespor.[11][12]
