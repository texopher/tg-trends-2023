iFootball club
i
iClube de Regatas do Flamengo, commonly known as Flamengo, is a professional
iwomen's association football club based in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Founded in
i1995, the team is affiliated with FFERJ and play their home games at Estádio
ida Gávea. The team colors, reflected in their logo and uniform, are red and
iblack. They play in the top tier of women's football in Brazil, the
iCampeonato
iBrasileiro de Futebol Feminino, and in the Campeonato Carioca de Futebol
iFeminino, the first division of the traditional in-state competition. Their
iplayers belong to the Brazilian Navy, which has formed a partnership with the
iteam in 2015.[1]
