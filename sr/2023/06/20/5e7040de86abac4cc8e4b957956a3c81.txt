i===== Early life =====
i
iGrowing up after his family moved back to Portugal, he joined the junior
iformative team of Frossos football club in 2001, but suffering a knee injury,
ihe was forced to quit a possible pro sports career.
i
iInstead of sports, Carreira concentrated more on an artistic career in music
iand fashion, particularly since he comes from a very artistic family. By this
itime, his father Tony Carreira was a famous singer who was well known by the
iPortuguese public and his mother Fernanda Antunes was his father's manager.
iHis sister Sara Antunes was already a public celebrity and his older brother
iMickael Carreira was having a singing career since 2007.
i
iDavid Carreira's first venture was as a model parading events such for
i"ModaLisboa" and "Portugal Fashion". He was featured in a number of
iadvertising campaigns, notably for Cacharel.
