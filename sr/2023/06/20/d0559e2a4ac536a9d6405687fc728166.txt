iDemon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a Japanese anime series based on the manga
iseries of the same title, written and illustrated by Koyoharu Gotouge. At the
iend of the second-season finale, a third season covering the "Swordsmith
iVillage" arc from the manga was announced.[1] The third season titled Demon
iSlayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Swordsmith Village Arc,[a] adapts volumes twelve
ito
ififteen (chapters 98–127) of the manga series which aired from April 9 to
iJune
i18, 2023.[2][3][4] The season was directed by Haruo Sotozaki, with character
idesigns by Akira Matsushima who also serves as a chief animation director,
iand
ianimation produced by Ufotable; the main cast from the second season also
ireturned.[1]
i
iOn February 3, 2023, a compilation film titled Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba
i– To the Swordsmith Village, which includes footage from the final two
iepisodes of Entertainment District Arc and an advanced screening of the first
iSwordsmith Village Arc episode, premiered in Japan.[2] Crunchyroll has
ilicensed the season, and began streaming an English dub on May 28,
i2023.[5][6]
i
iThe opening theme is "Kizuna no Kiseki" (絆ノ奇跡, lit. "Miracle of Bonds"),
iwhile the ending theme is "Koi Kogare" (コイコガレ, lit. "Yearning"), both
iperformed by Man with a Mission and Milet.[7] "Kamado Nezuko no Uta"
i(竈門襧豆子のうた, "Nezuko Kamado's Song") by Go Shiina featuring Nami Nakagawa is
ifeatured as an insert song in episode 11.[8]
